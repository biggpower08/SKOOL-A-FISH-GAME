Current implemented direction:
- SKOOL-A-FISH-GAME is a fish-school autobattler roguelite.
- The player manages fish. The player does not control sharks.
- Fish win rounds by surviving until shark hunger reaches zero.
- Fish lose if all fish are caught or school energy reaches zero.
- Shark hunger drains over time and restores when sharks catch fish.
- Starved sharks stop and show X eyes before the between-level screen.
- Shark speed is higher than basic fish speed by default.
- Starting shark attacks target roughly 18% of fish in the attack window.
- A level path preview shows current and upcoming level icons.
- Enemy HUD summarizes active shark types and hunger.
- Ambient Canvas ripples are always active around fish and sharks.

Current placeholder predator types:
- Basic shark: large dark circle, targets closest fish.
- Fast shark: dark circle with speed mark, faster chase.
- Pack-center shark: dark circle with center dot, targets school center.
- Barracuda/striker: thin dark oval, targets isolated outer fish.
- Eel: placeholder data exists for later line-pressure behavior.
